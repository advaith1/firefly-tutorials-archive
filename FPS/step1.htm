3D FPS Tutorial - Guide

1. A QUICK CATCHUP

Most Firefly games start the same way, so follow the Chocobreak 3D guide until you reach step 5 but instead of using the TropicalSunset skybox, we will be using the DesertSunset instead.

In the Chocobreak 3D guide we fixed the camera bluescreen issue by moving it back 100 - however for this guide just rotate the Z to -90, making the camera point forwards.

Once your game looks like this you can proceed to the next step.
